# SpringAI Hotel – Terms of Service

Welcome to **SpringAI Hotel**. These Terms of Service outline the rules and conditions that apply when booking and staying with us. By making a reservation, you agree to the terms below.

---

## 1. Booking Rooms

- Reservations can be made through our official website, mobile app, or authorized travel agents.
- Full payment is required at the time of booking unless stated otherwise.
- Please ensure that all personal details (e.g., name, ID information, contact details) are accurate. A correction fee of **€25** may apply for amendments.
- A booking confirmation will be sent to your registered email address within **24 hours** of booking.
- Room rates are subject to change until payment has been successfully processed.

---

## 2. Changes to Bookings

- Changes (except cancellations) can be made up to **24 hours before** your scheduled arrival.
- You can make changes online, through the mobile app, or by contacting our customer support team.
- Change fees apply based on room category:
    - **Standard Room**: €50
    - **Deluxe Room**: €30
    - **Suite**: Free of charge
- Same-day changes are subject to availability and may incur additional charges.
- If the new booking rate is higher than the original, you will be responsible for the fare difference.

---

## 3. Cancellations

- Cancellations are accepted up to **48 hours before** the scheduled arrival date.
- Cancellation fees apply based on room category:
    - **Standard Room**: €75
    - **Deluxe Room**: €50
    - **Suite**: €25
- Refunds will be processed to the original payment method within **7 business days**.
- Bookings made directly through SpringAI Hotel include a **24-hour risk-free cancellation period**.
- **No-shows** will be charged the full booking amount.

---

## 4. Check-in & Check-out

- **Check-in:** From 2:00 PM
- **Check-out:** By 11:00 AM
- Early check-in and late check-out are subject to availability and may incur additional charges.
- A valid government-issued photo ID and a credit card are required at check-in.
- Room keys must be returned at check-out; lost or unreturned keys will incur a **€50 replacement fee**.
